Inserting a model component

Getting Started ›› Working with Minsky ››

There are three ways to insert a component of a model onto the Canvas:

  1. Click on the desired Icon on the Icon Palette, drag the block onto the Canvas and release the mouse where you want to insert it

  1. Choose Operations from the menu and select the desired block there

  1. RIght-click on an existing block and choose copy. Then place the copy where you want it on the palette.
